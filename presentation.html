Let's Learn

How was your holiday? What did you do? What was the best part? In this lesson, you will practice describing past experiences, including good ones, bad ones, and everything in between.

Common Questions

Listen/read and repeat the following common questions.

  • How was your weekend / holiday / trip?
  • Where did you go?
  • What did you do?
  • How did it go?
  • Why? What happened?
  • Did you do anything fun / exciting / interesting?

👧

👦

👧

👦

Grammar: Participial Adjectives (-ed vs -ing)

SuffixRuleExample
-ing Use -ing if the noun is the reason or cause of the feeling. (Shows why a person feels a certain way. The subject is usually a thing.) This concert was boring.
-ed Use -ed to show the result or effect. (Describes how a person is feeling. The subject is usually a person.) She was disappointed.
